# Privacy Policy for Hide Promoted Jobs on LinkedIn

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026

## Introduction
"Hide Promoted Jobs on LinkedIn" ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and discloses information.

**In Short:** We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. The Extension operates entirely on your local device.

## Data Collection and Usage

### 1. No Personal Data Collection
We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal identifiable information (PII) or usage data on our servers. The Extension code runs locally on your browser.

### 2. Local Storage
The Extension uses your browser's local storage capabilities (`chrome.storage.sync` and `chrome.storage.local`) solely for the following purposes:
-   **Settings:** To store your preferences (e.g., whether the extension is enabled or disabled).
-   **Hidden Jobs:** To temporarily list the jobs that have been hidden during your browsing session. This data stays on your device and is not sent to us or any third parties.

## Third-Party Access
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. The Extension does not include any third-party tracking tools or analytics services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
